Offers a balance of all four skills: speaking, reading, listening, and writing. Consists of lectures on grammar, drill sections, oral and written exercises, aural comprehension exercises, and readings in elementary-level texts. First in a sequence of three. Offered: A.

BENG 200 Introduction to modern Bengali language, literature and culture (5) VLPA
Evolution of modern Bengali language, literature, and culture from the fifteen hundreds to the present. Topics may include the Bengali "renaissance"; the works of the Nobel Laureate Rabindranath Tagore, Nazrul Islam, and other writers; films of Satyajit Ray and other film-makers; or the emergence of Bangladesh. Texts in English.
View course details in MyPlan: BENG 200

BENG 201 Intermediate Bengali (5) VLPA
Develops proficiency in reading, writing, listening, and speaking standard colloquial Bengali at an intermediate level. Readings in fiction and non-fiction literature, vocabulary and grammar exercises, writing of essays and creative pieces, aural comprehension exercises, and topic-based conversation and role-play. First in a sequence of three. Prerequisite: BENG 103. Offered: A.
