URDU 424 Survey of Modern Hindi/Urdu Literature: Drama (5, max. 15) I&S/VLPA H. PAUWELS
Survey of Hindi-Urdu literature from the late nineteenth century to the present. Readings from representative dramas. The modern Hindi-Urdu theater that has developed in North India is important historically and, through its influence on the popular movies of Bollywood, forms an important part of contemporary popular culture of South Asia. Offered: jointly with HINDI 424; W.
